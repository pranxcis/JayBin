Workflow

Rotoscope Tutorial

Step 1

Choose Reference Footage

Select a clear video with visible movement and proper lighting.

Step 2

Trim Your Desired Clip

Cut the exact portion you want to rotoscope from the footage.

Step 3

Export Frame Sequence

Convert the video into image frames using DaVinci Resolve.

Step 4

Import to Rotoscope Software

Load the frame sequence into Adobe After Effects.

Step 5

Divide into Small Parts

Separate the animation into manageable 5-second sections.

Step 6

Identify Visible Objects

Separate body parts, clothing, accessories, and other elements.

Step 7

Trace Frame by Frame

Carefully trace movement while maintaining visual consistency.

Step 8

Freeze Completed Parts

Lock finished traced sections before moving to another part.

Step 9

Apply Colors and Matte

Add colors, textures, or designs to your traced objects.

Step 10

Export the Animation

Render the completed rotoscope sequence based on requirements.

Step 11

Refine Using Editing Software

Add text, audio, transitions, and additional media elements.

Step 12

Final Rendering

Export the finalized project and prepare for presentation.
